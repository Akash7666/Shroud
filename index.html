ABOUT SHROUD

Michael Grzesiek (born June 2, 1994), professionally known by his online alias Shroud,is a Polish-Canadian streamer and former Counter-Strike: Global Offensive professional. He is known for playing PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds(PUBG), Counter-Strike: Global Offensive

He helped lead Cloud9 to 2nd place in ESL One Cologne 2017 and a first place finish at ESL Pro League Season 4 in 2016

On October 24, 2019, Grzesiek officially announced his move from Twitch to Mixer and would be broadcasting exclusively on Microsoft's streaming platform, following the steps of fellow streamer Tyler "Ninja" Blevins, who announced a similar deal earlier that year.

He also participated in PlayerUnknown's Battlegrounds "Twitch Rivals" tournament, with his duo team placing second and winning a $5,450 prize
